What is Zestfestian?

Zestfestian is a constructed language created by 3 kids from South-East England. Every English letter maps to a Zestfestian letter. Learn the alphabet and you can write anything.... as long as you can remember it all lol

All 26 Letters

Vowels get an L prefix, and consonants shift to new symbols.

Three things to know

1

Vowels get an L in front

A becomes la, E becomes le, I becomes li, O becomes lo, U becomes lu.

2

Consonants shift to new letters

Each consonant maps to a new character. For example, B → Z, S → T', or N → E'. Apostrophes are a proper part of the alphabet. In fact, all of the consonants put together become "Zest Fest" and the reason the apostophies are there is because there was not enough letters for just zest fest so we had to improvise :)

3

Use the translator for anything

You don't need to memorise the whole alphabet. The translator converts English to Zestfestian and back, and even we use it because we can't remember it all
